Pulled Pork

Delicious Barbeque Glazed Pulled Pork

Whether you're making it in the slow cooker, oven, or smoker, we've got dozens of top-rated recipes for pulled pork.

Ingredients

  • Barbeque Sauce
  • Pulled Pork
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Mix the Barbeque Sauce and Pulled Pork Together
  2. Slice the Buns in the middle
  3. Put the Pulled Pork with Barbeque Sauce in the middle of the buns
